Search Commercial Building Energy Consumption Survey Buildings

This tool may be used to find approximate energy consumption for a building by searching for buildings in the 2003 CBECS with similar characteristics. The tool also finds the historic average commercial electricity retail price for the selected region from the U.S. Energy Information Administration.
